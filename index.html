Abstract

In this work, we present Chinese SimpleQA, the first comprehensive Chinese evaluation benchmark to assess the factuality ability of language models to answer short questions. Chinese SimpleQA features five main properties: (i) it's designed for the Chinese language across six major topics with a total of 99 diverse subtopics; (ii) it includes a comprehensive quality control process to ensure high-quality questions and answers; (iii) the reference answers are static and unchangeable over time; and (iv) it offers an easy-to-evaluate process since the questions and answers are short, leveraging the OpenAI API. By employing Chinese SimpleQA, we conduct an exhaustive evaluation of the factuality abilities of current LLMs. We hope that Chinese SimpleQA will aid developers in gaining a better understanding of the Chinese factuality capabilities of their models and support the advancement of foundation models.
